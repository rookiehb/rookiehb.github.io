Research Interests

I have broad interests in ML. Specifically, I am passionate about developing a trustworthy AI model with:

  • Interpretability: Understanding how AI models make decisions is essential for their acceptance. What I hope is to understand and quantitate the model's inference mechanism with strong theoretical guarantees, transforming a block-box model into explainable AI.
  • Generalization: An oracle model should utilize as less data as possible while enjoying super robustness to the unseen world, which requires domain-invariance and disentanglement.
  • Reliability: Models employed in real applications always encounter data that were not taught during training or suffer from malicious attacks, especially in the context of LLM today. This emphasizes the significance of reliable and safe AI.
